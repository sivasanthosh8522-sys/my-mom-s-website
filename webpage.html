About

Indra Rajkumar is a renowned bridal and celebrity makeup artist with over 10+ years of experience in enhancing natural beauty. Based in Tamil Nadu, she is known for creating elegant, flawless looks for weddings, receptions, parties, and special occasions.

With a keen eye for detail and a passion for perfection, Indra ensures every client feels confident, radiant, and truly camera-ready. Her signature style beautifully blends traditional charm with modern elegance, giving each bride a unique, luxurious finish.

She is also a proud member of BNI (Business Network International), showcasing her strong professional network and commitment to delivering high-quality service.
