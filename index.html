About

Traditional grace, modern finish, and a refined touch in every look.

Indra Rajkumar is a renowned bridal and celebrity makeup artist based in Tamil Nadu, known for creating elegant, flawless looks for weddings, receptions, parties, and special occasions. Her work blends precision with softness so every client feels naturally refined rather than overdone.

With over 10+ years of experience, she brings artistry, calm professionalism, and a sharp eye for balance, helping each client step into their moment with confidence and presence.

As a proud BNI member, she is part of a trusted professional network that reflects her commitment to reliability, meaningful relationships, and consistently high service standards.
